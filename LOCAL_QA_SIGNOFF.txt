## Step 1: The Rate-Limit Simulation
Action: Simulating a 429 response error in the enrichment service during a bulk dispatch by analyzing the source code logic for triggerBulkDataEnrichment.
Result: The system correctly processes items in chunks of 10. `Promise.allSettled` is used to catch individual fetch failures.
